About Us

Arlo Alchemyst s.r.o. is a European leader in the research and education of poisonous plants. Funded by public initiatives, we catalog toxic flora worldwide and educate communities on safety and prevention. Our workshops reach thousands of students each year, and our online resources make life-saving knowledge accessible to all. With science and education at our core, we work to build a safer, more informed world.

This webpage was created as a part of a long term school project for a computer science class.

The theme of the site centers around Arlo Alchemyst s.r.o., a fictional organization dedicated to the study and public awareness of poisonous plants.

This project was assigned to us to learn how to create our own webpage using HTML and CSS.
